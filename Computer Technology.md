**What is a Computer?**

Well, with the talk of AI everywhere, and having read and gained some understanding of what's really happening, I am kind of **thinking about a Computer in 4-5 different phases, each built on top of success of its previous state!**

**Phase 1: Calculation and Speed ->** A Computer was something humans could program for any algorithm. The Computer did that with ligthning speed which humans would never be able to. **Think of Book Keeping, Storing an entire movie and watching it anytime.**

**Phase 2: Network and Data ->** Humans connected the various computers of the world and allowed data to be shared. The Internet and servers enabled sharing and storing infinite knowledge. **Think of watching a Tennis match live without actually being there in the stadium or seamlessly booking a train ticket without physically going to the station.**

**Phase 3: Intelligence and Agentic ->** We are at the beginning of this phase. With consumer scale AI tools, Computers could now do tasks on behalf of us by building something called agents having specific skills. **Think of a future mobile phone automatically blocking calendar for making time for kids or notifying about a possible price increase of petrol.**

**Phase 4: Spatial and Cognitive ->** Eventually a Computer should become a robot doing laundary for us so that we could relax. **Eventually we could be wearing glasses and seamlessly switching between real physical world and digital spatial world**, watching movies, playing games, recording videos while travelling and talking to real people!

**Phase 5: Superintelligent and Savior ->** This is my naive optimism. Maybe if phase 3 and 4 succeed, **Computers of phase 5 would eventually help us solve climate change, poverty, governments, and diseases.** All of this while still having humans be somehow less than God or divine or whatever the supreme power there is!
